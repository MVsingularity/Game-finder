About Game Finder

Game finder is an online social media platform for competitive online gaming. Here you can earn money by playing in online tournaments, organizing matches between players and teams, and posting speedruns of games.

By creating an account, you are elligible to participate in events and directly challenge other players for sums of money. Your profile will include your rank (your ranks are different for each games), trophies and achievements you've earned, and a description of your skills and playstyle.

There is a fee for making an account (2$) and events with higher yields will most likely have an entry fee, but there are events that will be free and that will have monetary benefits.

Challenging other players will also reap monetary benefits. Challenging players of higher rank and winning will gain those players rewards but defending your place from lower ranked players will also grant great benefits. Money is given based on how far a member or team has climbed in rank and how battles a member or team has done to defend their rank in a month. Note: Rank will slowly go down if member does not participate in rank battles challenged from lower rank members(response time is extended for team battles)

Games will be chosen based on their active playerbase or if there is enough demand for it to be facilitated here.
